My Kind of Film

A Collection of My Favorite TV Shows

About My Favorite Shows

Here, I share my favorite TV shows and why I love them. These shows have inspired me in many ways and hold a special place in my heart.

Categories

Drama

Compelling narratives with complex characters and emotional depth:

"Breaking Bad"

Is a critically acclaimed American crime drama series following Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with terminal cancer, who turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine to secure his family's future, partnering with his former student Jesse Pinkman.

"Anne with an E"

Is a Netflix series that reimagines the classic coming-of-age story of Anne of Green Gables, focusing on a 13-year-old orphan girl's journey for love, acceptance, and her place in the world, set in the late 1890s.

Sci-Fi

Imaginative shows that explore futuristic concepts and alternate realities:

"Stranger Things"

Is a Netflix science fiction-horror series set in the 1980s, following a group of friends in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, who uncover supernatural mysteries, secret government experiments, and a portal to another dimension, known as the Upside Down.

"The Mandalorian"

Set in the Star Wars universe, follows a lone bounty hunter who is hired to retrieve "The Child" but instead goes on the run to protect the infant, encountering various adversaries and allies as he journeys through the lawless galaxy after the fall of the Empire.

Anime

Japanese animation with compelling stories, stunning visuals, and unique characters:

"Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation"

Follows a 34-year-old unemployed man who is killed in a traffic accident and reincarnated in a magical world as Rudeus Greyrat, retaining his memories from his previous life. With a second chance at life, he vows to live without regrets, using his knowledge from his modern upbringing and newfound magical abilities.

"Solo Leveling"

In a world where hunters with supernatural abilities must battle deadly monsters to protect humanity, Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest of all hunters, finds himself in a perilous dungeon where he nearly dies. After surviving a mysterious "System" program chooses him as its sole player, granting him the unique ability to level up and grow stronger with each battle, transforming from the weakest to the strongest.
