My TV Shows

A Collection of My Favorite Series

About My Favorite Shows

Television has the unique power to transport us to different worlds, introduce us to compelling characters, and tell stories that stay with us long after the credits roll. Here, I share my favorite TV shows across different genres that have captivated me with their storytelling, characters, and visual artistry.

Categories

Drama

Compelling narratives with complex characters and emotional depth:

Breaking Bad

Director: Vince Gilligan Stars: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul, Anna Gunn 2008-2013

A high school chemistry teacher diagnosed with inoperable lung cancer turns to manufacturing and selling methamphetamine to secure his family's future.

Anne with an E

Director: Moira Walley-Beckett Stars: Amybeth McNulty, Geraldine James, R.H. Thomson 2017-2019

A coming-of-age story about an outsider who fights for acceptance, for her place in the world, and for love.

K-Drama

Korean dramas known for their emotional storytelling, high production value, and cultural richness:

Crash Landing on You

Director: Lee Jeong-hyo Stars: Hyun Bin, Son Ye-jin, Kim Jung-hyun 2019-2020

A South Korean heiress accidentally paraglides into North Korea and meets a North Korean army officer who decides to help her hide.

Itaewon Class

Director: Kim Sung-yoon Stars: Park Seo-joon, Kim Da-mi, Yoo Jae-myung 2020

An ex-con opens a street bar in Itaewon to seek revenge on the family that caused his father's death.

Sci-Fi

Imaginative shows that explore futuristic concepts and alternate realities:

Stranger Things

Directors: The Duffer Brothers Stars: Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Winona Ryder 2016-Present

When a young boy disappears, his mother, a police chief, and his friends must confront terrifying supernatural forces in order to get him back.

The Mandalorian

Creator: Jon Favreau Stars: Pedro Pascal, Carl Weathers, Giancarlo Esposito 2019-Present

The travels of a lone bounty hunter in the outer reaches of the galaxy, far from the authority of the New Republic.

Action

High-intensity shows filled with thrilling fights, chases, and suspense:

Daredevil

Creator: Drew Goddard Stars: Charlie Cox, Vincent D'Onofrio, Deborah Ann Woll 2015-2018

A blind lawyer by day, vigilante by night. Matt Murdock fights the crime of New York as Daredevil.

Vikings

Creator: Michael Hirst Stars: Gustaf Skarsgård, Katheryn Winnick, Alexander Ludwig 2013-2020

Vikings follows the adventures of Ragnar Lothbrok, the greatest hero of his age, and his band of Viking brothers and family.

Anime

Japanese animation with compelling stories, stunning visuals, and unique characters:

Mushoku Tensei: Jobless Reincarnation

Director: Manabu Okamoto Stars: Yumi Uchiyama, Konomi Kohara, Ai Kayano (Voice) 2021-Present

A 34-year-old unemployed man is killed and reincarnated in a magical world as Rudeus Greyrat, where he resolves to live his new life without regrets.

Solo Leveling

Director: Shunsuke Nakashige Stars: Taito Ban, Genta Nakamura, Akio Ōtsuka (Voice) 2023-Present

In a world where hunters must battle deadly monsters to protect humanity, Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter, gains the unique ability to level up after surviving a deadly dungeon.

Comedy

Shows that bring laughter through witty dialogue, humorous situations, and memorable characters:

The Office

Creators: Greg Daniels, Ricky Gervais, Stephen Merchant Stars: Steve Carell, John Krasinski, Jenna Fischer 2005-2013

A mockumentary on a group of typical office workers, where the workday consists of ego clashes, inappropriate behavior, and tedium.

Brooklyn Nine-Nine

Creators: Dan Goor, Michael Schur Stars: Andy Samberg, Stephanie Beatriz, Terry Crews 2013-2021

Comedy series following the lives of detectives in Brooklyn's 99th precinct, led by the serious Captain Holt and the immature but talented Detective Jake Peralta.
